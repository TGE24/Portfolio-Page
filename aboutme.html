About Me
Idorenyin Obong

Idorenyin Obong (kingidee aka H.O.D) born on the 20th of October 1995 is a SoftWare Engineer, Google Certified Android Developer, Java & Kotlin, Technical writer based in Calabar Cross River State. I adore the creative process involved in the making of android apps, it's kinda addictive and the possiblities it gives are never ending, in essence i can say I LOVE WHAT I DO. (be thrilled to hear from you regarding an Android App Development Project). Programming has always been a favourite and 2017 was a big step up for me into the tech world.
